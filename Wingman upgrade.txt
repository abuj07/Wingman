9. LIVE CURRENCY FEEDS — SMART CACHING ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

FOREX FEED (TwelveData):
- Server-side only — the user's browser never calls the API directly
- Server fetches price and stores in a local Redis cache
- All users are served from the cache (10,000 users = 1 API call)

Dynamic fetch frequency logic:
  ACTIVE MODE   — User has interacted with the site in the last 5 minutes:
                  Fetch every 15 seconds, serve cached price to all users

  IDLE MODE     — No active users on site:
                  Drop to every 15 minutes to keep chart data gapless
                  without burning API quota

CRYPTO FEED (Binance WebSocket):
- Stays live and active as normal — no caching restrictions needed

HOMEPAGE TICKER STRIP:
- All instrument pairs scroll smoothly from RIGHT TO LEFT
- Premium-feel animated marquee — fluid, not jumpy
- Pairs ordered intentionally for a professional, premium feel

12. WINGMAN ACADEMY — 7-STAGE TRADER DEVELOPMENT JOURNEY
================================================================================

CORE PHILOSOPHY:
- The Sim Trader IS the classroom — AI grades real trades, not quizzes
- Progress is locked — each stage must be passed to unlock the next
- Every stage has a pass condition, a retry cool-down, and an AI debrief
- Academy Mode is clearly toggled separately from free-trade Sim Mode


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 1 — FOUNDATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Prove understanding of core trading concepts before touching the sim

Format: 5-question knowledge check (multiple choice + scenario-based)
Topics: Pips, lot sizes, leverage, spread, margin, basic candlestick patterns

Pass Condition: 4 out of 5 correct
On Pass: Sim trader unlocked with position sizing tools only (no live signals)
Addition: Glossary panel available and accessible throughout all stages


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 2 — RISK FIRST
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Demonstrate disciplined position sizing across 10 graded sim trades

AI grades on:
- Correct lot size relative to account percentage
- Stop loss placement in pips
- Minimum Risk:Reward of 1:1.5

Pass Condition:
- 8 out of 10 trades sized correctly
- No single trade exceeds 2% account risk

Failure Trigger: Any trade exceeding 3% risk = immediate stage reset
Addition: Risk calculator embedded in the trade panel during this stage


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 3 — READING THE MARKET
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Prove higher timeframe (HTF) bias alignment across 15 trades

Format:
- Before each trade, student declares bias (Bullish / Bearish / Neutral)
  and the timeframe they are trading from
- AI checks: Did entry direction match declared HTF bias?
             Was the HTF bias itself correct?

Pass Condition: 12 out of 15 trades with correct HTF bias alignment
Addition: Multi-timeframe (MTF) dashboard unlocked — shows D1, H4, H1
          bias summary to support the trader's decision making


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 4 — THE SETUP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Demonstrate confluence quality on 15 trades

AI scores each entry across:
- Market structure
- Trend alignment
- Indicator confluence
- Entry timing quality

Confluence score per trade: 0 – 100

Pass Condition:
- Average confluence score of 65 or above
- No more than 3 trades scoring below 40

Addition: Pre-trade checklist panel — student must tick confluences present
          before the entry can be submitted


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 5 — MANAGING THE TRADE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Prove trade management discipline across 20 trades

AI tracks:
- Did winning trades hit Take Profit or were they closed early?
- Did losing trades hit Stop Loss or were stops widened?
- Winner/Loser Hold Ratio (time held in winners vs time held in losers)

Pass Condition:
- Hold ratio above 1.2 (winners held longer than losers)
- Fewer than 3 early exits
- Zero stop widening events

Addition: Trailing stop tool unlocked at this stage for use in trades


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 6 — CONSISTENCY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Complete 30 trades with zero behavioural flags

Rules:
- All 6 behavioural detectors are fully active throughout
- Any flag pauses the stage and triggers an AI intervention message

Pass Condition:
- 30 trades completed
- Zero behavioural flags across all 30 trades
- Positive P&L (does not need to be large — just in the green)

Addition: Session journal auto-filled by AI after each trade
          Student can add their own annotations to each entry


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 7 — GRADUATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: The final assessment — comprehensive 50-trade review

AI generates a full TRADER PASSPORT including:

- Overall grade (A through F)
- Strengths and weaknesses profile
- Regime performance breakdown
- Behavioural profile summary
- Best trade annotated with AI commentary
- Worst trade annotated with AI commentary

SHAREABLE SCORECARD:
- Visual card (sports card style) displaying stats, grade, and Wingman badge
- Downloadable as PDF
- Shareable via unique link

LEADERBOARD OPT-IN:
- Anonymised ranking by graduation score
- Optional — trader can choose to appear or remain private
